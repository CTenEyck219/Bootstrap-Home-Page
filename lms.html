Learning Management System

What is a Learning Management System

A learning management system is a software application for the administration, documentation, tracking, reporting and delivery of educational courses or training programs

A cloud based LMS that empowers you to setup, deliver, and track learning all by yourself.

Course effectiveness rating

Evaluate the effectiveness of learning programs at increasing knowledge and capability and ensure they are delivering on business objectives. Assess the impact of your training by recording learner feedback, test scores and manager inputs. Improve content quality with real learner feedback.

Mobile learning and offline access

Now allow learners to resume learning even when they are online. The Adobe Captivate Prime app downloads learning content, makes it available for online access and auto-syncs when the learner is back online.

Powerful reports

Get actionable insights into your learning initiatives through personalized real-time dashboards. Keep tabs on how learners, departments, functional teams and partners are doing on their learning objectives.
